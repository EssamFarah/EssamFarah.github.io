About Me

My name is Essam, and this is my website. I am a sophomore at the College of Wooster, studying history, and hoping to work in museums. This website is a small showcase of my life at present, my past, and my hopes for the future. I am Lebanese, and grew up largely in Saudi Arabia, despite being born the United States. I hope you enjoy this project of mine.

My Life and Recent Work

Saudi Arabia

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia: The dry, dusty desert where I grew up. The scorching weather aside, things are not quite as bad as you might see on the news. To the surprise of some, there are actual cities there, more than just sand. Although there is plenty of that.

Lebanon

Lebanon is a beautiful country on the Mediterranean Sea, about one tenth the size of Ohio, and with about ten times the political issues. It is also where my family and I are from. Despite some of its issues, Lebanon is still a great place to visit, and has lovely, mild weather

The Warehouse

The Warehouses, whose owners' names will be kept private, was my first real job. I have done other work in the past, but it was all volunteer-based.I spent the majority of the time sorting and organizing parts for car transmissions, sweeping the floor, and crushing boxes. As tedious as it sounds, I did enjoy the on some level, and I am glad for the experience.

Current Projects and Goals

At the moment, most of my time is devoted towards studying. I am working towards a BA in history, and through that process have undertaken different projects and classes. In the fall of 2016 I co-authored and co-created an online museum exhibition on Omeka. Currently, I am working on a showcase of the history of one of the campuses's oldest houses: Miller Manor. Links to these projects are available upon request and are not linked in for the sake of my parteners.

The Future

The Future is, of course, a mystery. Nevertheless, I hope to work with public information, preferably a museum or library. These are crucial, and often undervalued pillars of our society that I hope to contribute to, and hopefully help as many people as I can learn through these mediums.

Outside of Work

Despite my time being consumed by various projects, I do have some time for myself. I have a 4th-Dan black belt in Taekwondo, and I enjoy reading, quiet walks, and an apundant level of bad puns.
